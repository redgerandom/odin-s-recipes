Description:

Simple mango smoothie recipe that is refreshing and easy to make. With four simple ingredients, it's the perfect healthy breakfast idea or sweet snack to add to your day. Any mango lover will agree that this mango smoothie recipe is one of the best!

Ingredients

  • 12.5 oz mango 2 cups chopped, fresh or frozen
  • 5.4 oz banana 1 large, fresh or frozen
  • 1.5 cup milk dairy or plant-based of choice
  • 1/2 cup natural yogurt

Steps

  1. If using fresh mango, first peel and dice the mango. For detailed directions, find out how to cut a mango here.
  2. Add everything to the blender. Make sure that the liquids are at the bottom
  3. of the blender and blend until smooth. It might take a couple of minutes to break up the frozen fruit. Be patient – it’s worth it!
  4. Once it's blended, pour it into your favorite smoothie glass and enjoy. Feel free to top with some shredded coconut or your favorite toppings.
