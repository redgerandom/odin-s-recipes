Going Green Avo Shake

Description:

Creamy and sweet, this milkshake is perfect for those afternoons when the temperature climbs past hot up to unbearable. Top it with whipped cream and toasted nuts, swirl in some chocolate syrup, add some fresh berries on top…the sky’s the limit!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ripe Avocado
  • 2 cups Baby Spinach (loosely packed)
  • 1 Frozen Banana
  • 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds (add more if preferred)
  • 1 cup Almond Milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon Maple Syrup (or stevia or honey)
  • ½ teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. To start with peel the avocado and remove the pit.
  2. Scoop out the flesh and into the blender along with rest of the ingredients and blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Do the taste test and adjust the sweetness or thickness as per you liking. You can add more maple syrup (or sweetener of choice) and milk if needed and blend again.
  4. Pour in glasses and serve
